Rock Chageability with Respect to % Sulphide Content
====================================================

Charging and integration times were about 1 minute each for these samples, which is much longer than field survey systems.
Therefore, values are much larger than field measurements.

+-----------------------+--------------------------+
|  **Material type**    | **Chargeability (msec)** |
+=======================+==========================+
| 20% sulfides          | 2000-3000                |
+-----------------------+--------------------------+
| 8-20% sulfides        | 1000-2000                |  
+-----------------------+--------------------------+
| 2-8% sulfides         | 500-1000                 |  
+-----------------------+--------------------------+
| volcanic tuffs        | 300-800                  |  
+-----------------------+--------------------------+
| sandstone, siltstone  | 100-500                  |  
+-----------------------+--------------------------+
| dense volcanic rocks 	| 100-500                  |  
+-----------------------+--------------------------+
| shale                 | 50-100                   |  
+-----------------------+--------------------------+
| granite, granodiorite | 10-50                    |  
+-----------------------+--------------------------+
| limestone, dolomite   | 10-20                    |  
+-----------------------+--------------------------+
