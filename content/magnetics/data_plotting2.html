Regional field.
Airborne magnetic data gathered a 25 square km
area around a mineral deposit in central British
Columbia. Some geological structural information
is shown as black lines. The monzonite stock in
the centre of the boxed region is a magnetic
body, but this is not very clear in the data
before removing the regional trend.

Local anomalous field.
Anomalous total magnetic field strength in
the boxed area of the large-scale map, after
the regional SW-to-NE trend has been removed.
Now the signature of the monzonite stock is
more clearly visible.
