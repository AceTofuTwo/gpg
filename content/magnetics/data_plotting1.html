Total field strength is measured along six lines covering an area of 15 x 50 metres. With no susceptible material underground, all values would be the same (about 56,000 nT near Vancouver, BC.)
Values recorded will vary if susceptible material exists. These variations in total field strength can be displayed as a contour plot.
Filling the contour plot helps visualize the magnetic field variations.
Colour contour maps are now the preferred form of plotting raw "total field intensity" data.
Data along one line is often plotted as a graph in order to display more details (click here ).
